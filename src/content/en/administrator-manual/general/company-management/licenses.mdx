# Licenses

### Overview

The Licenses page allows you to check the currently active product license information in QueryPie.

<figure data-layout="center" data-align="center">
![Administrator &gt; General &gt; Company Management &gt; Licenses](/administrator-manual/general/company-management/licenses/screenshot-20240804-184804.png)
<figcaption>
Administrator &gt; General &gt; Company Management &gt; Licenses
</figcaption>
</figure>

*  **License Status** : Active, Expired, Waiting (license waiting to be activated after existing license expires)
*  **Product Name** : Displays the currently used QueryPie product name
*  **Usage Period** : Shows start and end dates
*  **License Type** : Production (official product license), PoC (PoC license)

<Callout type="info">
If your license is about to expire or has expired, please contact us through the [QueryPie Customer Portal](https://help.support.querypie.com/) or your sales representative.
</Callout>
